From Execution to Evolution

Option Auditor is an open-source intelligence engine designed for the serious retail trader. We don't just list your trades; we reconstruct them.

By seamlessly ingesting raw data from Tastytrade and Interactive Brokers, our engine groups disparate order legs into complex strategies (Iron Condors, Verticals) and runs institutional-grade risk checks. Whether it's monitoring Average Daily PnL or flagging over-leverage, our goal is simple: turn your raw trading data into a roadmap for consistency.
